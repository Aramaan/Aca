Researcher, Author and Speaker
 
 

Speaking.

 

Justifying why research is important can be difficult.

This is especially true when dealing with blue-skies research where applications and commercial routes are in the distant horizon. Meanwhile, blue-skies research is often the most exciting and most ambitious kind of projects one can work on.

A trade-off! It’s therefore pertinent that we plan, optimise, and clearly communicate our ideas and efforts to the various stakeholders (public or private).

 
 
 

World Haptics Conference Cross-Cutting Challenge - Montreal (Virtual) 2021

 
 

This workshop addressed Covid-19 pandemic challenges that stimulated an overhaul of ultrasound mid-air haptics towards touchless tactile interfaces in public spaces.

Workshop Chair: Orestis Georgiou - Ultraleap Ltd

 
 

 IT Weekend - Kiyv 2018

 
 

Gesture based control interfaces are becoming ubiquitous in our daily lives but lack physicality and feedback. In this presentation, I talk about how recent developments in ultrasound technologies have bridged this gap and made it possible to combine gesture interfaces with haptic feedback.
